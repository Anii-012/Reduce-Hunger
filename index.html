What is Hunger?

Hunger is a condition in which a person, for a sustained period, is unable to eat sufficient food to meet basic nutritional needs.

Why is it Necessary to Eliminate Hunger?

Eliminating hunger is crucial for improving public health, reducing poverty, and promoting global stability and peace.

How to Reduce Hunger?

Reducing hunger involves a combination of efforts including improving food security, increasing agricultural productivity, and addressing social inequalities.
